# Sprite Generation Prompts

You need 12 images: 6 poses for each of your 2 pets. Use an AI image tool (ChatGPT with DALL-E is free) to generate them.

## How to use these prompts

1. Open [ChatGPT](https://chatgpt.com) (or any AI image generator)
2. Copy a prompt from below
3. Replace the placeholder text (in CAPS) with your pet's actual description
4. Paste into ChatGPT and generate the image
5. Download the PNG and save it to the `sprites/` folder with the filename shown

## Before you start

Decide on your pets' descriptions. For example:

- **Pet 1:** "a golden retriever with a red bandana, fluffy golden fur"
- **Pet 2:** "a small black and white border collie puppy, speckled markings"

Or for cats, hamsters, rabbits — anything you like! Just describe their fur color, markings, and any distinctive features.

## Tips for better results

- **Generate all 12 in one session** so the art style stays consistent
- If the first image looks good, tell the AI "keep this exact same style for the next one" before pasting the next prompt
- If the background isn't transparent, ask: "Please regenerate with a transparent background"
- Download as PNG (not JPG) to keep the transparent background
- If your pet has unique features (floppy ears, a spot on the nose), add those details to YOUR_PET_DESCRIPTION
